Powered by

We are incredibly excited to announce that our 2D adventure scroller is under development, for accessibility reasons we have opted for a web based game which can be played on both mobile and desktop.

To ensure our wider target market are encouraged to participate in the game we have decided to make it PLAY-TO-EARN! This means that every single player will have a financial incentive to play no matter what.

Players will earn in a variety of ways including completing levels, defeating bosses and collecting in-game power ups! The in-game power ups will offer various multipliers to an individual's earnings and will be entirely randomised to further motivate our community to keep playing.

On top of this, we will hold competitions, during which the top three players of the day will be rewarded. We believe this will incentivise and motivate players to play every day. This will not only increase our exposure but also build more hype and bring more community members!

Other projects have implemented this feature with resounding success and we plan to be the next. The Shaman King-Inu side scroller will be developed in Unity and will have an NFT element, these NFT's will be in the form of power ups, equipment and characters!

Users will be incentivised to collect these NFT's to increase their chances of winning the daily competitions and getting to the top of our leaderboard!

We hope you're all as excited as we are to play our game. We've been working hard on this and we want to ensure that we live up to the expectations of the community.

As a community orientated project we always want to hear from you! If you have any feedback, ideas or suggestions, feel free to drop by our Telegram group or Twitter and let us know!

Shamans Roadmap

Phase 1

Phase one was orientated around the development of our smart contract and community building. As a team, we strive for organic, natural growth to shake off the paper hands and pump-and-dumps. To nurture the authenticity of our community, we got our project audited by safe solidity. We also focused on slow and steady growth, relying on calls from trusted Telegram and Twitter marketers with strong followings. Through this, we established a strong foundation of community members who diamond-handed us throughout the dips to every all time high when listed on Uniswap.

Phase 2

In this phase, we will be focused on actively listening to the community we built, asking what they wanted and molding our token based on their feedback. We understand the importance of utility, therefore in this stage we would be developing our game to incentivise the community to not only stay active, but continue to engage with our token.

Phase 3

Following the launch of our custom-built game, we would be developing NFTs which would find use in this game. We will then continue to focus heavily on marketing to spread the word about our token that has evolved from a community-led project to an Ethereum project with established use case, solid team and a diamond-handed community. We want to reward our early investors by continuing to push our token to the stratosphere. We will continue to shape Shaman King Inu based on the ideas put forward by our community and put Shaman Inu in the same category as the likes of Floki, Spell and other tokens that evolved from humble beginnings to household names in the DeFi space.
